Website Critiques

Questions

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they hace links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphis/layout/multimedia enhance the site or distract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Critique of Trader Joe's

www.TraderJoes.com

  1. I like the website. It has nice photos and icons. I do think it is a tiny messy though.
  2. I think the whole website is a little too much at once and is organized funny. If I was a customer I wouldn't know where to go to find what I wanted. I also think the side bar should have been put horizntal.
  3. Like I said before it is a little too much and can be confusing.
  4. Thegraphis/layout/multimedia definitely take away. At first it looked great and simple but then I realized it was too much when trying to navigate. It is just too much. I think there needs to be more blank space.
  5. The load time was very quick. I didn't have to wait at all.
  6. Like I said at first it seemed simple but then I realized it was too much. I think the background photo is what makes it so distracting. Maybe they should have made it more transparent.
  7. The navigation bar is okay. I have to go and click on the thinks and look at the page to understand what each one really was.
  8. The site has a color scheme but you would think it was a different one than it is for the whole website. The photo has a whole colo scheme that doesn't match the rest of the website. There is a vintage style through the whole website.
  9. The site works just fine with smaller screens.
  10. I like that once you go through the website and look at the other pages it is easier to navigate and read. It is just the homepage that was too much.
